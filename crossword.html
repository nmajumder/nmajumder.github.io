Welcome to my interactive crossword puzzle application, where you can attempt to solve some of the puzzles I've made. They are in the style of (and abiding by the rules of) New York Times crosswords, some being 15 by 15 and some 21 by 21. Each will alert you when you correctly complete it, and will let you know when you fill the entire puzzle if there are mistakes left to fix. You can check your answers or get help with specific words and spaces if you get stuck. Click on one of the puzzles below to begin. Have fun!
